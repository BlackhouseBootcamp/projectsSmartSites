Overview

Each of our spacious, light-filled rooms has everything you could possibly need for a comfortable stay, including free Wi-Fi, Apple iPads, Etro bedding, L'Occitane and Jo Malone products. There are even laptops in certain rooms.

Of course, achieving comfort isn't our only goal. We value forward design as well. The rich hues of nature's color pallet are highlighted by clean lines, modern furnishings, and an abundance of white, which can be seen from the sea-view windows and terraces of our suites
